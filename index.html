Take no tomodachi ni naritai!
The uncontrollable spreading of bamboo is viewed as a challenging matter owing to its invasive nature in the forests. Often, people would choose to abandon the land or cut down the bamboo massively. However, we would like to shift the focus from ignoring the situation to taking advantage of bamboo's fast-growing capability in supplying sustainable support to food forests such as converting them into composts, mulches, etc. Not just that, introducing food forest into a bamboo forest would improve the overall forest diversity which will be beneficial for keeping forest's productivity and stability. Lastly, food forests can potential be a source of income to the people taking care of them.
